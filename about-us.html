Basic Essay


What is essay about?

A basic essay is a given task that every student at school and university makes. An essay must be well-structured because your ideas must be clear and concise, Look the charts below, there you will have material to study more about essays.

Structure

In the introduction, you should write background information or context about the topic you are writing about, remember you do not have to write all your ideas in there. Also, you should state your position and one paragraph is perfect to do it.

(Main ideas): In this part of the essay, you must give all the information about the chosen topic,the main points about it and show examples, details and evidence to make it interesting and real.

In addition, two or three paragraphs are adequate to make a strong body.

Conclusion: To conclude you must restate the main information and make a close with a powerful sentence or you can add a famous phrase that has something in common with the essay.

Opinion Essay


Tips

Tip #1

Introduce each paragraph with a topic sentence, outlining the main ideas.

Tip #2

Write in formal style

Tip #3

Do not use short forms.

Tip #4

Do not use colloquial expressions.

Tip #5

Do not write about advantages or disadvantages or points for or against about your opinion.

  • What is an opinion essay about?

    In an opinion essay you should share your opinion about a topic, the objective of this essay is persuading the readers with your point of view.

    Structure
    Introduction

    In the introduction, you should write background information or context about the topic you are writing about, remember you do not have to write all your ideas in there. Also, you should state your position, write about if you agree or disagree with the topic. One paragraph is perfect to do it.


    Body

    In this part of the essay, you must give your whole opinion, the main points about it, but show examples and details to make it interesting.


    Conclusion

    To conclude you must restate your opinion and make a close with a powerful sentence.

    An email

    Email is a digital communication method that uses electronic devices to deliver messages. For writing is an essential part to put it in practice, you have to learn how to write one because it will be useful when you are applying, requesting, ask for something important by email. Also, it is important because it is part of a writing test.

    How to create an email:
    To start: who is your email for? Write the email address or name of the person who you would like to send the email.

    Subject line: short sentence that summarizes the content of your email.

    Gretting: be formal and kind

    Body: Main paragraph of your email that communicates main message. Use proper grammar and punctuation and be sure to capitalize when needed.

    Closing: short words or sentences that indicate that you finished, use formal and friendly vocabulary

    Your name: It shows who wrote the email

    a pros and cons essay


    A pros and cons essay is a type of argumentative essay that encourages you to look at both the pros (positives) and the cons (negatives) of a given topic. You may have this essay well-structured and formal to analyze the main issue of the topic.


    Structure:

    Introduction: Begin your essay by introducing your topic and explaining that you are exploring the advantages and disadvantages of this topic. we may use any of the following techniques to make the beginning more interesting to the reader:

    -a reference to an imaginary scene or situation
    - a quotation
    -A one paragraph would be perfect for this part.

    Body:The main body usually consists of two paragraphs. One presents the points for the issue, along with our justifications or examples. The other presents the points against the issue with supporting sentences.

    Closing: In the final paragraph we give our opinion and/or a balanced summary of the topic. To make the ending of the composition more effective, we can use:

    - state a personal opinion
    - give the reader something to consider
    - use a quotation or rhetorical question
