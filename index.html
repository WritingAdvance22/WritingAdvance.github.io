Skills for Writing

Research

Before you write a single word, you need to do your research about the topic you are writing on.

Planning or outlining

Creating and following an outline ensures you’re incorporating all the important information in the right order and not being repetitive or straying too far from your point.

Grammar and Clarity

make your writings clear, concise, and ready to read by others, have the grammar structures well- written and check if your writing communicates effectively

Revising and Editing

You can revise or edit by making significant changes to the structure, organization, or content of a piece.

Pros and Cons Essay

  • Essay

    The essay is a written piece that is designed to present an idea, argument, propuse and express an opinion,
    the essay has to be well-structure and formal.

    Max words: 250 to 650 Max time: 30 min Do it with the vocabulary that you know.
    Lesson

  • Opinion Essay

    The opinion essay is a formal piece of writing where you demostrate your opinion about a topic.

    Your opinion essay should be clar showing reason, point of view and arguments bout that topic, also we must show evidence of hy you opine in that way.


    Max words: 250 to 650 Max time: 30 min Give evidence of your opinion.
    Lesson

  • Email

    e-mail is a message sent from one computer to another over the Internet, using some applications to send it by an address. One part of the TOEIC Writing test is to respond to an email, which structure you will find it in our web page.

    Max words: 50 t0 150 Max time: 20 min Be formal
    Lesson

  • Pros and Cons Essay

    A pros and cons essay is a type of argumentative essay that encourages you to look at both the pros (positives) and the cons (negatives) of a given topic. Your essay, however, should not just be a list of pros and cons.
    Also this type of essay provides to the readers different points of view that can help them about a topic.

    Max words: 250 to 50 Max time: 45 min Be organize with the ideas.
    Lesson
